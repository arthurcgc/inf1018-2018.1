As funções big_val, big_sum, big_sub e big_mul funcionam para numeros signed e unsigned.
alguns dos testes feitos:

TESTE 1:
big_val(a,2)-> a=0x02000000000000000000000000000000
big_val(b,2)-> b=0x02000000000000000000000000000000
big_sum(res,a,b)-> sum=0x04000000000000000000000000000000
big_sub(res,a,b)-> sub=0x00000000000000000000000000000000
big_comp2(a)->  0xfeffffffffffffffffffffffffffffff
big_mul(res,a,b)-> a*b=0x0400000000000000f800010101010101

TESTE 2:
big_val(a,2)-> a=0x02000000000000000000000000000000
big_val(b,2)-> b=0x03000000000000000000000000000000
big_sum(res,a,b)-> sum=0x05000000000000000000000000000000
big_sub(res,a,b)-> sub=0xffffffffffffffffffffffffffffffff
big_comp2(b)->  0xfdffffffffffffffffffffffffffffff
big_mul(res,a,b)-> a*b=0x0600000000000000f800010101010101
